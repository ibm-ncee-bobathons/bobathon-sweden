# How to Sign Up for a watsonx Orchestrate Trial Account

IBM watsonx Orchestrate offers a **30-day free trial** with full access to all Standard edition features.

---

## What You Get

- 30 days of free access to all Standard edition features
- Ability to build, deploy, and manage AI agents
- Access to 700+ pre-built integrations
- Invite up to **10 team members** (as builders or users)

---

## Sign Up via IBM Cloud

### Step 1 – Create an IBM Cloud Account

If you don't have an IBM Cloud account yet:

1. Go to [cloud.ibm.com/registration](https://cloud.ibm.com/registration)
2. Fill in your email address, name, password, and country
3. Click **Create account**
4. Verify your email address via the confirmation link IBM sends you

> If you already have an IBMid or IBM Cloud account, skip to Step 2.

### Step 2 – Go to the watsonx Orchestrate Catalog Page

1. Log in at [cloud.ibm.com](https://cloud.ibm.com/login)
2. Navigate to the [watsonx Orchestrate catalog page](https://cloud.ibm.com/catalog/services/watsonx-orchestrate)
   - Or search for **"watsonx Orchestrate"** in the IBM Cloud catalog

### Step 3 – Select the Trial Plan

1. On the catalog page, select **Trial plan**
2. Choose your preferred **data center location** from the *Select a location* dropdown
3. The **Service name** is pre-filled — you can change it if needed
4. The resource group is set to **Default**

### Step 4 – Create Your Instance

1. Accept the license agreement
2. Click **Create**
3. Wait for the instance to provision — this takes a few moments

### Step 5 – Access watsonx Orchestrate

1. On the services page, click **Launch watsonx Orchestrate**
2. You're in! A link to your instance is also sent to your registered email

## Launching watsonx Orchestrate from IBM Cloud

Once your instance is provisioned, here is how to get back to it at any time:

### From the Resource List

1. Log in at [cloud.ibm.com](https://cloud.ibm.com/login)
2. Click the **Navigation menu (☰)** in the top-left corner and select **Resource list**
3. Expand the **AI / Machine Learning** section
4. Find your **Watson Orchestrate** instance and click on it
5. Click **Launch watsonx Orchestrate**

### From the IBM Cloud Catalog

1. Go to the [watsonx Orchestrate catalog page](https://cloud.ibm.com/catalog/services/watsonx-orchestrate)
2. If your instance is already provisioned, IBM Cloud will direct you to your existing service
3. Click **Launch watsonx Orchestrate**

### Directly via Email Link

After provisioning, IBM sends a direct access link to your registered email address. Bookmark this link for quick access going forward.

> **Tip:** Once inside watsonx Orchestrate, click **Trial days left** in the top bar to see how much time remains on your trial.
